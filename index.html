📘 About Airbnb

Airbnb was born out of a simple yet revolutionary idea in 2007, when two designers, Brian Chesky and Joe Gebbia, opened their San Francisco apartment to three guests in need of a place to stay during a sold-out design conference. This humble beginning sparked a global movement, combining exceptional design thinking with the power of community. Today, Airbnb stands as a beacon of innovation and creativity, with over 5 million hosts welcoming more than 1 billion guests around the world.

At its core, Airbnb redefines the way people connect and explore the world, blending functionality and aesthetics to create extraordinary travel experiences. For design-focused product managers, Airbnb represents a unique opportunity to shape the future of human-centered design at scale. Our platform is a living canvas, where every pixel, interaction, and feature is thoughtfully crafted to delight users, empower hosts, and build trust within a global network of travelers.

Airbnb's DNA is rooted in design excellence—from the intuitive simplicity of our booking flow to the personalized experiences powered by cutting-edge AI. As a design-focused product manager, you’ll collaborate with world-class designers, engineers, and researchers to push boundaries and solve complex problems with elegance and empathy. Together, we’ll craft seamless user journeys that inspire exploration, foster belonging, and elevate the role of design in shaping human connections.

If you’re passionate about combining data-driven insights with creative vision, and you’re ready to lead the charge in designing the future of travel and hospitality, Airbnb is your stage. Join us to create meaningful, impactful products that empower people to live anywhere and feel at home everywhere.

🌍 The Community You Will Join

Airbnb's commitment to community is at the heart of everything we do. This ethos drives us to continually innovate and improve how we connect people and foster belonging worldwide. By integrating cutting-edge technology with our deep-rooted commitment to community, Airbnb continues to set the standard for meaningful connections in the global travel ecosystem. Together, we’re shaping a future where everyone, everywhere, can truly belong.
